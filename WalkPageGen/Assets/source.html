This is the web site of The Walking Club,
a Peterborough based hiking group.





{ShortcutTable}

If you wish to take part in any of the listed walks then let Alan know by e–mail 48 hours before the walk so that we can arrange cars.

If subsequently you find that you cannot make it, please give us a ring or text and let us know. If this is on the morning of the walk then ring Alan’s mobile; telephone numbers are in the newsletter.

Our main meeting place in Peterborough is the car park opposite The Woodman pub which is on Thorpe Road just down from Thorpe Road Police Staion PE3 6SQ (latitude:longitude = N52°34’4.46”:W0°17’28.02”). For walks where we travel north up the A1, a second meeting place is at the Danish Invader Pub in Stamford PE9 2SX (latitude:longitude = N52°39’13.66”:W0°30’04.93”) as indicated in walk details below. For Lincolnshire walks we sometimes by arrangement have a meeting place in Market Deeping which is the public car park on Halfleet PE6 8DU (latitude:longitude = N52°40’56.26”:W0°19’30.87”). Click on the links for maps of the meeting points.

You should bring a packed lunch unless stated otherwise. Please bring a change of shoes so that you do not get mud over the cars.

We may split into groups on the day if there are sufficient numbers and suitable walk leaders. But one group will always do the advertised walk.

Routes can be obtained in advance from Paul or Alan.

On Walking Club weekends away, if there is a suitably experienced member of the Club who can not make the weekend but would like to lead an alternative local walk, then please contact Alan in advance and he will publicise it.
